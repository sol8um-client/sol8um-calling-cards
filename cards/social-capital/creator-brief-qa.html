System 03 · Creator Brief & Draft QA Engine · Day 7-10 of every launch

100 creator briefs, in their voices.
Then auto-QA before you read them.

The creator-coordination layer is where launches go to die. Generic briefs go out, mediocre drafts come back, the Head of Growth spends 8 hours reading drafts where 70 of them needed the same edits. This system generates per-creator briefs tuned to each creator's voice and best-performing format, then auto-scores every draft against virality patterns. The Head of Growth sees only the drafts that already cleared the bar.

How it runs

STACK
Launch deck input → n8n + Claude per-creator brief generation (using each creator's last 30 posts as voice reference) → creator submits draft → Claude QA against pattern library (from System 02) → Head of Growth review of pre-filtered drafts.
REPLACES
Sending the same generic brief to 100 creators. Reading 100 drafts manually with most needing the same notes. The Head of Growth burning Day 8-9 on draft review instead of strategic decisions.
ANCHOR METRIC
Manual draft-review time per launch (target: under 2 hrs, was 8 hrs). Drafts hitting publishable bar on first submission (target: 60%+, was ~25%).
