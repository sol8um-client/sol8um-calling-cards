System 04 · Launch Distribution Orchestrator · Launch day, first 24 hours

The system runs the calendar.
You make judgment calls.

Launch day is where 500 creators across 12 timezones either compound or fall flat. The Head of Growth's worst case is spending launch hour 0 in Slack DMing creators. This system runs the posting calendar, monitors first-90-minute engagement signals, triggers amplification cascades when a launch is hot, and pivots when it's cold. The Head of Growth and the founder both watch the same dashboard. Decisions get made on numbers, not vibes.

How it runs

STACK
Posting schedule (Postgres) → engagement webhook (X / LI APIs) → n8n decision engine → Slack alerts to Head of Growth + creator network → live launch dashboard (Next.js / Retool).
REPLACES
Head of Growth in Slack on launch day messaging "are you posting yet?" Founder asking "how's it going?" every 30 min. The "we missed the amplification window" failure mode where a hot launch flatlines because the cascade didn't trigger fast enough.
ANCHOR METRIC
Time from launch-go to peak-reach (target: under 4 hours, was 12+). Manual coordination hours per launch day (target: under 2 hrs, was 8+).
