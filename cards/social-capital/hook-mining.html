System 02 · Viral-Hook Mining System · continuous, daily refresh

Every launch starts from a fresh library,
not from memory.

The Head of Growth's daily worst-case is "I scrolled X for 2 hours and found 3 hooks." This system replaces that with a continuously-refreshed library of every post in your corner (AI, Tech, VC, Startup) that crossed 1M views in the last 90 days. Tagged by hook type. Searchable by category, by reach, by engagement ratio. Open the library, pick the lens, see 50 fresh hooks ranked by recent performance.

How it runs

New high-performing hooks discovered per week (target: 30+). Hook-library reuse rate per launch (target: 50%+ of starting hook ideas come from library).
