## 5. The 4 BESPOKE workflows (each tied to "What You'll Do Here")

### Workflow 01: Founder-Voice Extraction Engine (the launch-video starting point)
- **What it is:** ingests founder calls + sales call transcripts + product demo recordings; uses LLM extraction to surface aha moments, surprise statistics, emotional peaks, customer-voiced pain
- **Output:** rank-ordered list of "hook candidates" with verbatim quotes + suggested 30-60-second storyboard draft
- **Anchor metric:** time from "founder call recorded" to "storyboard draft ready" — target <90 minutes (was: a week of manual review)
- **Replaces:** "Watch 4 hours of founder calls and pray you spot the hook." The Head of Growth opens the queue, not the calendar.
- **Stack:** Whisper / AssemblyAI (transcript) → n8n + Claude (extraction + ranking) → Notion / Figma board (storyboard draft) → manual creative direction layer on top

### Workflow 02: Viral-Hook Mining System
- **What it is:** continuously scrapes X / LinkedIn advanced search for posts in their corner (AI / Tech / VC / Startup) that crossed 1M views in last 90 days
- **Tags:** by hook type (statistic / contrast / emotional / format / giveaway / curiosity-gap), by industry, by reach, by engagement ratio
- **Output:** searchable hooks library that the Head of Growth opens at the start of every launch — "what's worked recently for product launches in our category"
- **Anchor metric:** new high-performing hooks discovered per week; hook reuse rate per launch
- **Replaces:** "I scrolled X for 2 hours and found 3 hooks." Now: open the library, filter by category, see 50 fresh hooks ranked by recent reach.
- **Stack:** X / LI advanced search APIs → n8n daily crawler → Postgres/Supabase → Claude tagging pass → Retool dashboard

### Workflow 03: Creator Brief & Draft QA Engine
- **What it is:** for the 100+ creators per launch — auto-generates personalized briefs per creator's voice / audience / past-best-performing-format from the launch deck. Then auto-QAs each draft against virality patterns (hook strength, length, CTA presence, payoff structure) BEFORE human review
- **Output:** every creator gets a brief in their voice; every draft gets a virality score + specific suggestions before the Head of Growth sees it
- **Anchor metric:** manual draft-review time reduced ~70%; creator brief turnaround <2 hours
- **Replaces:** Head of Growth spending 8 hours per launch reading 100 mediocre drafts. Now they see only the 30 that already cleared the QA bar.
- **Stack:** launch deck input → n8n + Claude per-creator brief generation → creator submits draft → Claude QA against pattern library (from Workflow 02) → human review of pre-filtered drafts

### Workflow 04: Launch Distribution Orchestrator
- **What it is:** coordinates the 500+ creator network's posting across timezones for a single launch. Monitors early engagement signals (first 90 minutes). Triggers amplification cascade (signal-boosting partners, paid amplification, creator re-engagement) if hot. Triggers pivot/pause if cold.
- **Output:** a launch-day dashboard the Head of Growth + founder both look at; system makes calendar/sequencing decisions, human intervenes only on judgment calls
- **Anchor metric:** time from launch-go to peak-reach; manual coordination hours per launch
- **Replaces:** Head of Growth spending launch day in Slack messaging 100 creators "are you posting yet." The system knows.
- **Stack:** posting schedule (Postgres) → engagement webhook (X / LI APIs) → n8n decision engine → Slack alerts to Head of Growth + creator network
