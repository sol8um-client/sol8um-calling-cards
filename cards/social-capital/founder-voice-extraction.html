System 01 · Founder-Voice Extraction Engine · Day 0-1 of every launch

Four hours of founder calls go in.
Twelve ranked hooks come out.

The single most expensive bottleneck in launch-video production is the manual hunt for the aha moment. A Head of Growth listens to 4-6 hours of founder calls, sales calls, and demo recordings, takes notes, and prays they spotted the hook. This system runs that hunt in 90 minutes. The output is a rank-ordered list of hook candidates with verbatim quotes, plus a first-draft storyboard. The Head of Growth opens the queue, not the founder's calendar.

How it runs

STACK
Whisper / AssemblyAI (transcript) → n8n + Claude (extract aha moments, surprise stats, customer-voiced pain) → Notion / Figma board (ranked hook list + storyboard draft) → manual creative direction layer.
REPLACES
The Head of Growth listening to 4 hours of founder audio with a notebook. The "I forgot to flag the best line" failure mode. Round 4 of the storyboard rewrite because the core hook was wrong.
ANCHOR METRIC
Time from "founder call recorded" to "ranked hooks ready." Target: under 90 minutes. Was: 1-2 days of manual review.
