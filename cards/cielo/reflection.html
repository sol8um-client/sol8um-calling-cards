Workflow 04 · Reflection Layer · Sundays + monthly

Self-coaching,
quietly automated.

Three weekly questions delivered Sunday evenings. A voice memo if you'd rather speak. A monthly review that shows you where your hours actually went, what energized you, what drained you, and which conversations changed something. The tasks-to-state ratio of your month, made legible.

Cadence

Weekly — Sunday 7pm. 3 questions. Type or voice. ~7 min.
Monthly — 1st of the month. Auto-generated review tying time-on-calendar, drained-vs-energized signals, decision quality. Ready for you, or for your founder coach.

Why this exists

Most founders don't burn out from work. They burn out from not noticing the cost of work. This layer makes the cost legible weekly so it never compounds silently.
