Theme A
First glance & AI-search visibility
01

Your meta description is the Figma Make default placeholder.

Critical · 5 min fix

Hidden in your <head> tag. Visible everywhere CIELO is referenced — Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, link previews on LinkedIn and Slack. This is what every cold prospect reads before they decide to click.

google.com/search?q=miami+brand+agency
www.cielo.agency
CIELO Agency Branding and Brand Management
Currently: "This web application showcases blog posts with an intuitive design, featuring hover effects on titles and buttons for enhanced user engagement."
www.cielo.agency
CIELO · Brand strategy for founders and investment firms
Suggested: "Brand strategy and design for founders, investment firms, and category leaders. Miami-based. AI-native. Engagements that compound."
Cost to ICP
A PE managing partner who pastes cielo.agency into ChatGPT today reads about hover effects, not perception. The most expensive real estate in your funnel is misrepresenting your agency for free.
Quick win
Open Figma Make page settings → SEO → Description. Replace. Five minutes. Compounds across every search citation, every share, every cache for the next quarter.
Impact Critical · effort 5 min

Theme D
Conversion engine & revenue capture
09

Three competing CTAs above the fold. "Discovery" is consultant-speak.

Medium · 30 min fix

You have Free Signup, Discovery →, Download Pricing, and Set a Call all visible above the fold. Three different "next steps" makes the visitor pick which path is for them. The good news: each CTA is well-targeted. The bad news: in combination they leak conversions.

cielo.agency LIVE · 4 CTAs ON ONE SCREEN
CTA #1 · FREE SIGNUP
CTA #2 · DISCOVERY →
CTA #3 · DOWNLOAD PRICING
CTA #4 · SET A CALL ↗
Quick win
Two CTAs above the fold. "Download Pricing" (lead-magnet path) + "Talk to Kiana" (call path). Drop "Discovery" — it's vague, it's consultant-speak, and the founder name + photo do more conversion lifting than a button label ever will.
Impact Medium · effort 30 min
10

Reb2b reveals which firms visit. Apollo enriches them. There's no outbound loop.

Strategic · biggest revenue lever

Your site runs Reb2b (B2B visitor identification), Apollo (form enrichment), Contentsquare (heatmaps), GoHighLevel (CRM), TWIPLA (visitor analytics), and GA. That stack identifies which companies hit your site even if no one fills a form. That's worth the most as outbound fuel — but only if there's a follow-up loop.

Quick win
An n8n workflow that listens to Reb2b webhooks, filters for ICP-matching domains, enriches via Apollo, drafts a personalized first-touch email referencing the page they viewed, and queues for your approval. Built in two days. This single workflow could be worth more than the entire EA role.
Impact Strategic · effort 2 days

Why ten findings, not ten quick fixes

Each one compounds. Together they change the funnel.

Directional model. Most agency-funnel improvements stack non-linearly because they remove drag at every stage. Even half of them shipped means meaningful inbound delta by month three.

Where the lift comes from
M1: meta + hero ship to existing traffic. M2: trust stack + landing pages convert better-fit visitors. M3-4: Reb2b → outbound loop adds net new conversations.
What it costs
Most of M1-M2 is hours, not dollars. M3-M4's outbound loop costs the n8n + Apollo seats you already have. The audit is essentially free; the loop pays for itself in the first qualified conversation.
