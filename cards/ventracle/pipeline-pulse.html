A dashboard we both look at.
No "how's it going" check-ins.

The reason demand-gen relationships break down is opacity. Founder asks "how's it going?" Operator says "good, busy." Founder believes them until month three when nothing's working. This dashboard removes the question entirely. Demos booked, reply rate by channel, sequence health, deliverability, ICP filter performance. Both partners look at it. Decisions get made on numbers, not vibes.
