System 01 · ICP-Locked Lead Sourcing · 06:00 daily

30 to 50 high-fit leads, queued
before you sit down.

The only thing worse than no demand engine is a demand engine that pumps generic lists. This system pulls fresh leads from Apollo and Sales Nav daily, dedupes against your CRM, scores by ICP fit, and queues the top 30 to 50 in a sequence-ready state. The filter retrains every week on which leads actually closed, not just which replied. So the queue gets sharper every month, not just bigger.

How it runs

STACK
Apollo + Sales Navigator + Clearbit enrichment → n8n dedupe + ICP scorer (Claude-assisted) → CRM queue (HubSpot or Pipedrive) + Slack #queue-ready morning ping.
REPLACES
Hiring a list-builder VA. Buying static lists from a vendor every quarter. Founder spending 2 hrs/week on Apollo.
ANCHOR METRIC
Reply rate of leads with fit-score ≥70 vs. <70. Target gap: 3-5x. Improves monthly through retraining.
