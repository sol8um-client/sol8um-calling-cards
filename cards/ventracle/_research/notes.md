## 7. The 4 BESPOKE workflows (each = something I'd own as the partner)

### Workflow 1: ICP-Locked Lead Sourcing
- **What it is:** A daily-running stack (Apollo + Sales Nav + dedupe against existing CRM) that drops 30-50 high-fit leads into the queue every morning
- **When it fires:** Every morning at 6am CST (his time)
- **Who owns it:** Me (the partner)
- **Compounding edge:** Weekly retraining of the ICP filter based on which leads actually book demos and which close
- **Replaces:** "Hire a list-builder VA" or "buy a static list from a vendor"
- **Mock data shape:** Lead row → company name, domain, contact name, role, fit-score (0-100), source signal, enrichment status, ready-to-sequence flag

### Workflow 2: Voice-Matched Outbound Sequences
- **What it is:** Email + LinkedIn + voice sequences written in HIS voice (we workshop the copy together day one), A/B-tested live, with reply-aware branching
- **When it fires:** Continuous — sequence sends ~50 touches/day across channels, replies pause the sequence per-lead
- **Who owns it:** Me (operator), him (voice and approval)
- **Compounding edge:** Every variant tested for statistical significance, not vibe. Best variants get promoted; losers get pruned. The sequence gets sharper monthly.
- **Replaces:** "Hire an SDR who'll send 50 manual emails a day with whatever copy they default to"
- **Mock data shape:** Sequence variant → step type (email/LI/voice), copy version, send time, reply rate, demo-booked rate, win-rate downstream

### Workflow 3: Reply Triage → Demo Booking
- **What it is:** Every reply lands with me first. I classify (interested / not now / not a fit / objection), respond appropriately, book demos via his calendar, and brief him pre-call with the prospect's full context (company, role, signals, fit-score, what they replied to)
- **When it fires:** Every reply, in real time. Pre-call brief auto-generated 30 min before each demo.
- **Who owns it:** Me (triage + booking + brief), him (the demo itself)
- **Compounding edge:** He walks into demos already warmed up. He doesn't read the inbox. His 100% close rate stays intact because his time is protected.
- **Replaces:** "Founder spends 90 min/day in inbox" or "founder shows up to demo cold without context"
- **Mock data shape:** Reply → classification, response sent, demo booked Y/N, pre-call brief generated Y/N

### Workflow 4: Founder-Level Pipeline Pulse
- **What it is:** A shared dashboard we both look at daily. Demos booked (today / this week / this month), reply rate by channel, sequence health (which variants are winning), cold infra deliverability, ICP filter performance
- **When it fires:** Continuous; both partners check at start-of-day
- **Who owns it:** Both (transparency is the whole point)
- **Compounding edge:** No "how's it going" check-ins. The data is in front of both of us. Decisions get made on numbers, not vibes. Trust compounds.
- **Replaces:** "Founder has to ask the SDR every 3 days for an update" or "founder finds out about demand-gen problems 2 weeks after they started"
- **Mock data shape:** Daily metric tile → value, 7-day trend, alert thresholds, drill-down to underlying records
