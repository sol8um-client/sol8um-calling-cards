System 03 · Reply Triage to Demo Booking · continuous, real-time

Every reply lands with me. You walk into demos
already warmed up.

The single biggest reason founders burn out on outbound: 90 minutes a day in the inbox triaging replies, sending follow-ups, manually booking demos. This system catches every reply, classifies it, sends an appropriate response, books the demo on your calendar, and auto-generates a pre-call brief 30 minutes before each meeting. Your 100% close rate stays intact because your time stays protected.

How it runs

STACK
Smartlead inbox + Gmail webhook → n8n classifier (Claude) → Cal.com / Calendly booking + Notion pre-call brief generator + Slack #demos-incoming.
REPLACES
Founder spending 90 min/day in inbox. Demos booked without context. The "I forgot what they replied to" failure that breaks rapport.
ANCHOR METRIC
Founder inbox minutes per booked demo. Target: under 2. (Just reading the pre-call brief.) Was: 5-10 min triaging plus reply.
