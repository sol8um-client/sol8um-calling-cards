✨ Bright spot cohort
PhD candidates (STEM, year 2-4)
34% activated on Agent this week (+8pp vs last). Usage pattern: literature review first, then grant draft. Average session: 47 min. Credits re-purchase rate: 62%.
⚠ At-risk cohort
Postdocs (STEM, any year)
9% activation (-5pp vs last). 61% purchased credits, 0 Agent tasks completed within 7 days. They buy, open the Agent, and close it. Something in the onboarding step loses them.
2 experiment hypotheses for next week
Hypothesis A: Postdocs aren't activating because the Agent's first-run UX defaults to "literature review" -- a task they do differently than PhD students (they supervise, not write). Show a "systematic review" task type on first run. Metric: postdoc 7-day activation rate.
Hypothesis B: Credits confusion is causing postdocs to open Agent, see an empty prompt, and close without trying. Add a "Try this: [suggested research question]" pre-fill on first run. Metric: first-task completion rate for credits holders.
